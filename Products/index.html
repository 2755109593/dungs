Product portfolio

DUNGS - the interface between gas and safety. We understand the complete range of technologies for controlling, metering and modulating of combustible gas and combustion air. We offer the complete range of hardware: From single valves to complete gas trains and from automatic burner controls to complete burner management systems. Internationally successful product families guarantee reliability, efficiency and user-friendliness that is proven around the world.
